The Great Escape

I won't be doing Instructional Videos on You Tube, or promoting Lecture Series, on becoming a Designer. However, I can give the brief (albeit, unrecommened) technique, and the way home, I took.
Growing up, my additude was not so ordinary ... I didn't KNOW I couldn't fly
So I did
I hacked a Guy Clark song
As natural law goes, and it always does ... You're flying until you hit the ground!
FACT ... I HIT THE GROUND
Uncomfortable. I needed a plan.
Drawing and Art, seemed innate; natural to me, and were drawing me in again (no pun ) and becomes the Subject Matter. Historically, and statistically, both are beggars' Vocations. I kept balking. These weren't attractive, nor driving, forces.
However, after my fortune-teller told me I had no more Last Chances, I entered Ryerson at the age of 26
The choir gave me a loud AMEN
Needless to mention, as well ... my mother thanked the Heavens with profusion
In my terms, my decision provided contentment ... On another level, contentment might not be a good Pay Master
